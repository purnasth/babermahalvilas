Kathmandu to Dubai Flight

The Kathmandu to Dubai flight is one of the most popular international routes, connecting two vibrant cities. This flight takes about 4-5 hours and provides easy access to the Middle East for work, tourism, and business. Most flights are non-stop, offering a comfortable journey with stunning aerial views of the Himalayas and the desert as you approach Dubai.

Price: Rs. 50,000 per person

Kathmandu to Singapore Flight

Flights from Kathmandu to Singapore are a gateway to Southeast Asia's bustling urban and cultural hub. This route is widely popular among travelers heading for both leisure and business. With a flight duration of approximately 5-6 hours, the journey promises comfort and a smooth experience across Asia’s international airlines.

Price: Rs. 45,000 per person

Kathmandu to Bangkok Flight

A Kathmandu to Bangkok flight offers travelers a quick and easy way to explore Thailand's rich culture, beautiful beaches, and bustling city life. The flight duration is about 3-4 hours, and with regular, affordable flight options, it's a preferred route for both tourists and business travelers heading to Thailand.

Price: Rs. 40,000 per person

Kathmandu to Kuala Lumpur Flight

The Kathmandu to Kuala Lumpur flight offers a convenient way to explore Malaysia's capital, known for its modern skyline and rich cultural heritage. With a flight time of approximately 4-5 hours, this route is frequently traveled by business and leisure travelers looking to enjoy Malaysia’s diverse offerings.

Price: Rs. 48,000 per person
