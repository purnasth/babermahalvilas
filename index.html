A time capsule of Nepalese Architecture. An amalgamation of the best of Nepalese architecture. Baber Mahal Vilas, an architectural museum !

In an effort to portray the distinct architectural features of Nepal in an essentially neoclassical 'Rana' style.

Luxury and Service, in a haven of history, culture and tradition, Baber Mahal Vilas is a boutique hotel in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal.

The hotel is rich in historic significance to its location, built upon the grounds of the Baber Mahal Palace, to reflect its grandeur.

Baber Mahal Vilas

The vision behind the boutique property is to create a hospitality model where in, luxury and service are provided in a historically and culturally significant ambience in the center Kathmandu Valley.

The History

The property is built and run by the descendants of the longest ruling prime minister of Nepal Maharaja Chandra Shumsher Rana and his son Baber Shumsher Rana who was the Commander and Chief of the Nepali Armed Forces.

The property

Baber Mahal Vilas - The Heritage Hotel is a quaint family-run boutique property set in the existing architectural haven of Baber Mahal Revisited. In an effort to portray the distinct architectural features of Nepal in an blend of architectural design, the property shows 4 distinct styles.

Rooms

Experience The Rana style of living

Traditional Room

These rooms are compact and yet inviting, reflecting the skilled craftmenship of Newari Artisans. The rooms are adorned with traditional art and vibrant handwoven textiles, adding warmth and colour.

Traditional Premium Room

These rooms reflect the diverse architectural elements of the Rana era as well as the local Newari and Buddhist architectural features. Rooms are spacious and look over the quaint courtyards of the property.
