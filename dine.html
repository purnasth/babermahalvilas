We specialize in the authentic Nepalese Durbar cuisine at Baithak Restaurant & a small quiant bar, K2 Terrace Cafe, offering a wide selection of wines, spirits and cocktails.

Baithak Restaurant

An all day dining inside the hotel premises. Offers delicious Nepali cuisine catered to our clients with an ambience of tradition and heritage. The menu can be enjoyed either in the restaurant area or on the roof top swimming pool area with a view of the surrounding mountains.

Baithak Restaurant is a great place to have a meal. The food is delicious and the service is excellent. The staff is very friendly and helpful. The atmosphere is very nice and the view is beautiful. I would definitely recommend this place to anyone who is looking for a great place to eat in Kathmandu.
