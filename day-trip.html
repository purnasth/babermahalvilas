Kathmandu Valley Tour

Discover the cultural heart of Nepal with a full-day tour of Kathmandu Valley. Visit iconic landmarks such as Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), Boudhanath Stupa, and Pashupatinath Temple. Dive into the rich history and architectural marvels that make Kathmandu a UNESCO World Heritage site. This guided tour offers deep insights into the city’s religious and cultural heritage.

Price: Rs. 3,500 per person

Bhaktapur Day Tour

Take a trip back in time with a day tour of Bhaktapur, an ancient city that is home to some of Nepal’s most stunning temples, palaces, and courtyards. Marvel at the intricate wood carvings and brick architecture that have stood the test of time. Key highlights include Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Nyatapola Temple, and the 55-Window Palace.

Price: Rs. 4,000 per person

Chandragiri Hill Tour

Escape the hustle and bustle of Kathmandu with a trip to Chandragiri Hill. Take the cable car up the hill to enjoy panoramic views of the Kathmandu Valley and the majestic Himalayan ranges, including Mount Everest on a clear day. The hilltop also houses the Bhaleshwar Mahadev Temple, making it a perfect blend of natural beauty and spirituality.

Price: Rs. 2,500 per person

Dakshinkali and Pharping Tour

Explore the spiritual sites of Dakshinkali and Pharping, located just outside of Kathmandu. Start your journey with a visit to Dakshinkali Temple, dedicated to the Hindu goddess Kali, known for its vibrant religious ceremonies. Continue to Pharping, a renowned Buddhist pilgrimage site where you can visit several ancient monasteries and caves where Guru Rinpoche meditated.

Price: Rs. 3,200 per person
