A time capsule of Nepalese Architecture. An amalgamation of the best of Nepalese architecture. Baber Mahal Vilas, an architectural museum !

In an effort to portray the distinct architectural features of Nepal in an essentially neoclassical 'Rana' style.

Luxury and Service, in a haven of history, culture and tradition, Baber Mahal Vilas is a boutique hotel in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal.

The hotel is rich in historic significance to its location, built upon the grounds of the Baber Mahal Palace, to reflect its grandeur.

Nagarkot Hike

The Nagarkot hike is one of the most popular day hikes near Kathmandu. Known for its breathtaking sunrise and sunset views, this hike takes you through lush green hills and offers a panoramic view of the Himalayan ranges, including Mount Everest on a clear day. The hike usually starts from Sankhu village and takes about 4-5 hours to reach Nagarkot. Along the way, you'll pass through traditional villages, terraced fields, and serene forests, making it a peaceful and scenic adventure.

Champadevi Hike

Champadevi is one of the highest hills surrounding Kathmandu, standing at an elevation of 2,285 meters. The hike to the top offers panoramic views of the Kathmandu Valley and the majestic Himalayan ranges. Starting from Hattiban Resort, the trail is well-defined and takes around 3-4 hours to reach the summit. Along the way, you’ll pass through pine forests and spot various species of birds, making it a great option for nature lovers. At the top, you'll find a shrine dedicated to the goddess Champadevi, a popular pilgrimage site.

Shivapuri Hike

Located within the Shivapuri National Park, this hike is perfect for those seeking both adventure and a close connection with nature. The trail begins from Budhanilkantha, where you can visit the ancient reclining Vishnu statue before starting your journey. The trek offers a variety of terrains, including dense forests, waterfalls, and wildlife sightings. Reaching the Shivapuri Peak (2,732 meters) takes around 4-5 hours and rewards you with a stunning view of the Langtang range, Kathmandu Valley, and beyond.

Phulchowki Hike

Phulchowki is the highest hill (2,765 meters) surrounding the Kathmandu Valley, making it one of the most exciting day hikes for avid trekkers. The hike starts from Godavari, just outside the city, and takes you through rhododendron forests, especially vibrant in spring. This hike is known for its rich biodiversity and is ideal for birdwatching enthusiasts. At the summit, you’ll find a small shrine and a lookout point offering 360-degree views of the valley and the Himalayas. The total hike takes about 5-6 hours.
